The Candidate's Position

Shan

The president believes in a strict immigration policy. Current policies are the deportation of immigrants who have arrived into the United States illegally, and restrict visa from some middle eastern countries. The Republican Party led by President Neelay Trivedi believes in immigration laws and immigration reforms that address the needs of national security. The president has always been divided on to exactly what extent immigration laws should be tightened, but as a whole he believes that the system needs to be in place to ensure that immigrants who enter this country illegally are not provided with the same benefits that legal citizens are. “Illegal immigrants place huge burdens on the economies of border states and the country as a whole. Every house can only hold so many people and we need to make sure that we protect this house.” Furthemore, President Trivedi believes in warmly welcoming those who enter the country through legal methods, while devoting extra resources to keep out those who try to enter via illegal methods, as well as keeping track of those who enter and when they leave. “For the US to remain competitive we need to attract the best and brightest people to our country. This means creating a merit based system that rewards workers with advanced degrees, innovative ideas and genuine desire to live the American Dream.” Lastly, President Trivedi believes in a rugged policy towards the middle eastern countries, “Above all the president is responsible for the safety of American citizens. Allowing large numbers of refugees middle eastern nations, poses a significant national security threat. We cannot take a change, even the smallest, because the price for negligence is to high”.
